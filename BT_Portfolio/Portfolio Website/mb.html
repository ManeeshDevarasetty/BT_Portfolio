MOVIE BLOG

The Pursuit of Happyness

Christopher Gardner stakes everything on a medical device he believes will secure his future—the bone density scanner. But reality hits hard when the device proves difficult to sell. His financial struggles drive his wife away, and soon, he and his son are left homeless. Determined to turn his life around, he earns an unpaid internship at a brokerage firm, where he must outperform the competition over six months to secure a job. With no income and a son to care for, Gardner fights against the odds, holding onto hope with unwavering determination.

Catch Me If You Can

A prodigy of deception, Frank Abagnale Jr. orchestrated a multimillion-dollar fraud, living as a pilot, physician, and attorney—all before 19. An FBI agent makes it his mission to end Frank’s spree, but the young trickster relishes the pursuit.

Saving Private Ryan

During the Allied invasion of Normandy on June 6, 1944, Captain Miller and his men from the 2nd Ranger Battalion struggle to establish a foothold. Amidst heavy casualties, two brothers lose their lives, adding to the loss of a third who had perished earlier in New Guinea. Back in the U.S., Mrs. Ryan is about to receive three death notifications in a single day. Recognizing the immense burden, Army Chief of Staff George C. Marshall orders a rescue mission—eight men are tasked with finding and safely returning her last surviving son, Private James Ryan.

Up

Since childhood, Carl Fredricksen has dreamed of reaching the mysterious Paradise Falls. More than six decades later, he takes off on an airborne adventure, his house soaring into the sky with the help of thousands of balloons. Alongside him is Russell, a persistent Boy Scout. As they encounter new allies, including a talking dog, Carl soon realizes that danger lurks ahead. The mastermind behind it? His once-beloved childhood idol.

Rocky IV

Apollo Creed takes on the fearsome Ivan Drago, but the fight takes a deadly turn. When Creed loses his life in the ring, Rocky Balboa is left heartbroken—and furious. Fueled by vengeance and guided by Duke, he travels to the Soviet Union, ready to take on Drago in the most legendary fight of his career.
